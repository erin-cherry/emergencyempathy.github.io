Emergency Empathy

You're not alone in your struggle. You deserve validation and empathy when navigating difficult moments. Consider this website your empathy buddy, for whenever you need to be heard, to take a pause, or to transform your thinking.

The phrases are randomized, however, our hope is that we're able to meet you where you're at - in joy, frustration, curiosity, grief, and the spaces in-between.

We're all just doing the best we can, given our current circumstances.

If you're in crisis and need support from a person, these resources may be helpful:
